Chakapuli: Georgian Herb-infused Lamb Stew

Chakapuli is a classic Georgian lamb stew featuring a medley of fresh herbs like tarragon, cilantro, and mint.

Simmered with onions, garlic, and white wine, it offers a fragrant and flavorful taste of Georgian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Lamb chunks
  • Fresh tarragon, cilantro, and mint
  • Scallions
  • Onions and garlic
  • Dry white wine

Recipe Instructions

  1. Season lamb chunks with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Allow to marinate.
  2. Saut√© finely chopped onions until translucent, then add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Brown marinated lamb on all sides in the pot for enhanced flavor.
  4. Pour in dry white wine, let it simmer to reduce, then add water to create a flavorful base.
  5. Stir in finely chopped tarragon, cilantro, mint, and scallions. Simmer until the lamb is tender, adjusting seasoning as needed. Enjoy!
