Khinkali

Dumpling in Georgian cuisine, made of twisted knobs of dough, stuffed with meat, and spices.

The fillings of khinkali vary with the area.

The original recipe, the so-called khevsuruli, consisted of only minced meat (lamb or beef and pork mixed), onions, chili pepper, salt, and cumin.

Ingredients

  • Dough/flour
  • eggs
  • ground beef and pork mix with (red pepper, ground caraway seed, onions - optional and water).

Recipe Instructions

  1. Add flour to a mixing bowl. Make a depression in the middle of the flour and add the eggs.
  2. Add warm water.
  3. Mix the ingredients from the middle of the bowl until all of the flour is mixed.
  4. Divide the dough into two pieces, start kneading and folding until the dough is very firm.
  5. Roll out the dough until it is at desired thick, and cut out circles.
  6. Mix with meat and use your thumbs and index fingers to make an accordion type fold all around the outside.
  7. Carefully place the dumplings into a deep pan of boiling salty water, about 10-15 at a time (depending on the size of your pan).
